{{#if dendrogram}}

Overlays a dendrogram onto the projection. The hierarchical level of the clusterings is reflected by the thickness of the lines, with thicker lines indicating closer, lower-level clusters.

The dendrogram is computed using agglomerative clustering with a Ward linkage criterion in the high-dimensional space.

{{/if}} {{#if errordisplay}}

Display the projection error (stress1) for each point in relation to all other points. Hovering over a point shows error relation to that specific point.

  • other points appear farther away, yet are actually more similar
  • other points appear closer by, but are actually not that similar
  • the size of the halo shows the relative magnitude of the error
{{/if}} {{#if clustercontrol}}

Sets the number of clusters for the clustering algorithm.

The clusters are computed using agglomerative clustering with a Ward linkage criterion in the high-dimensional space.

{{/if}}
